on neuroscience

Books

  • The Book of GENESIS: a free book on neuroscience simulations in the context of the GENESIS simulator. Very readable introduction to computational neuroscience irrespective of the simulator
  • Wulfram Gerstner’s book

Courses

  • Wulfram Gerstner’s course at EPFL
  • Robert Sapolsky’s lectures on Human Behavioral Biology

Simulators

  • MOOSE (Multi-scale Object Oriented Simulation Environment): a general purpose simulation software for biology with focus on systems biology and neuroscience simulations. Inherits a lot from the GENESIS simulator.
  • NEURON: another simulator with primary focus on neuronal models; added some systems biology capabilities in recent years.

Databases

  • Open Source Brain: a public repository of neuronal models
  • neuromorpho.org: a public repository of neuronal morphologies
  • NeuroElectro: a public database of electrophysiological properties of various neurons
  • Allen Brain-Map: multiple databases, from single cells to brain atlas
  • Micron explorer: Allen Brain Institute portal with electron-microscopic reconstructions of mouse visual cortex circuitry
